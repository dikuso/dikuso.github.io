Schedule a Meeting Request

Please fill out your details and preferred meeting time. This will generate a Google Calendar event link for you to send an invitation.

Important:

  • After clicking "Generate Google Meetup Link", a new Google Calendar event page will open.
  • **You MUST manually set your email address (`YOUR_EMAIL@gmail.com`) in the JavaScript below** where it says `const myEmail = 'YOUR_EMAIL@gmail.com';`. This is where the meeting invitation will be sent.
  • Review the pre-filled details, add guests (like your own email), and send the invitation from Google Calendar.
  • The Google Meet link will be automatically generated by Google Calendar.
